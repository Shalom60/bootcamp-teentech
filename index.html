Sign up for a 3-Days coding boot-camp, Operated by TeenTech Africa.

The Bootcamp is gaired towards female tech enthusiast who are fired up to learn to code. It focuses on the foundations of web development, and seeks to foster a balanced ratio between guys and girls in tech.

About The Bootcamp

The bootcamp is a complete crash course, and everything needed to get up and running with HTLM5, CSS and JAVASCRIPT. By completeling the course work contained in the bootcamp, along with practice, you will be well on your way into the world of web development.

Who Should Apply

Only girls can apply. The bootcamp focuses on participants with no prior knowledge either by going through a training phase, or self learning. Irrespective of the previous fact, girls with previous trainings who seek to rekindle their passion for software development are encouraged to register and attend the bootcamp. Only 20 slots is available for registration.
